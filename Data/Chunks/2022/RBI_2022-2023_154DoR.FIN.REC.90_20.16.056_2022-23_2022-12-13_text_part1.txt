## **Data Format for Furnishing of Credit Information to Credit Information Companies and other Regulatory Measures**

Please refer to the [circular DBOD.No.CID.BC.127/20.16.056/2013-14 dated June 27, 2014,](https://rbi.org.in/Scripts/NotificationUser.aspx?Id=8968&Mode=0) *inter alia* setting out a Uniform Credit Reporting Format for reporting credit information to the Credit Information Companies (CICs).

2. It is clarified that cases admitted with National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT)/National Company Law Appellate Tribunal (NCLAT) under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code, 2016 are also required to be reported under the suit-filed cases in reporting to the CICs.

3. Credit Institutions (CIs) shall ensure implementation of this circular latest by February 28, 2023.
