3. It is clarified that investments in special securities received from the Government of India towards bank's recapitalisation requirement from FY 2021-22 onwards shall be recognised at fair value / market value on initial recognition in HTM. The fair value / market value of these securities shall be arrived on the basis of the prices / YTM of similar tenor Central Government securities put out by Financial Benchmarks India Pvt. Ltd. (FBIL). Any difference between the acquisition cost and fair value arrived as above shall be immediately recognized in the Profit and Loss Account.

## **Applicability**

4. This circular is applicable to all Commercial Banks (excluding Regional Rural Banks).
